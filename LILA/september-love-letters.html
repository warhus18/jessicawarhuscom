Gibson & Birkbeck

As the weather turns crisper, we're looking to our newest designer, Gibson & Birkbeck, to refresh our seasonal wardrobes and keep us looking on point! Established in Scotland, the brand's signature line of ladies button-down shirts offers a range of charming wildlife print motifs that are perfectly prep and make for great transitional pieces as we edge into fall. Pair your classic print with a trendy leather skirt if you're heading to the office, or layer it under a cozy pullover and booties on chillier days.

We've got four different woodland prints available in store and in the web shop: Blue Jay Feather (pictured), Mallard, festive Blue Finch or the Sly Fox. Or, for more a relaxed off-duty style, choose the slouchy tee in navy and brown pinecone.

5 Ways to Living Healthier

For the most part, we all try to live healthfully. That is, whenever we can remember to commit. We make countless plans, resolutions and promises that we presume will be life-changing, only to put them off for just one more night, for one last hurrah.

A few years ago, I made the conscious decision to stop regarding healthy living as simply an option - one that I could choose to follow whenever it was convenient for me - and instead embraced it as part of my lifestyle. At the risk of sounding too preachy (eek!) let me say I've fallen off the bandwagon many, many times. I by no means possess the discipline of a monk, the endurance of an Olympic athlete or the enlightenment of a yogi. I'm very much so, a work in progress. That being said, I thought I would share 5 things on the blog that I changed about my routine, which allowed me to live a healthier and more satisfying life.
