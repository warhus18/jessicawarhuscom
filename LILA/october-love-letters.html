LILA Scots Collection

Tartan is back by popular demand this season and we spotted it on every fashion blogger, social darling and celebrity that snapped their way from New York to London, Milan and Paris for Fashion Month this past September. This classic print, hailing from the Highlands, draws us back again and again with its Ivy League flavor and incredible versatility.

Our cozy LILA Scots Collection, made from 100% pure new wool in Scotland, brings you the season's must-have Mini Kilts and sophisticated Ladies Day Kilts as well as cozy scarves and ultra modern Tammy Hats. Choose from a variety of statement patterns that speak to your personality, including the Thompson Dress Grey, Thompson Camel (pictured), Macbeth Modern, and festive seasonal prints like the Stewart Royal and Princess Mary Modern. Pair your picks with a comfy turtleneck and chunky jewels. No matter how you style it, tartan is the perfect essential for your runway inspired, ready-to-wear look this season!
