Grecian Chic

Fashion is art - don't ever let anyone tell you otherwise. One of our favorite designers at LILA, Elena Zournatzi of Grecian Chic, so perfectly illustrates this thought with her oh-so-dreamy silk scarves. Marrying Greek design with impeccable British craftsmanship, each one of Elena's 100% silk twill scarves tells a story inspired by her homeland of Greece and her travels to other cosmopolitan cities around the globe. Follow her heroine, the Grecian Chic lady from London to Paris and back to the Greek Village she calls home, in a series of charming depictions that inspire nothing short of a grand getaway.

Elena studied at the prestigious Central St. Martins, the world-renowned arts & design school where fashion icons Alexander McQueen and Dior's John Galliano are alums. She was also honored this past summer with designing the cover of 2board, the official Athens Airport magazine (left). She hand illustrates all of her designs before digitally printing them in color on silk! How's that for a standout accessory?

The Scarf Game

Everyone has that go-to accessory. The one thing you grab to add that pop to your look, to make it feel more you. My staple item has always been the silk scarf. Whether it be new or vintage, solid or patterned, my closet is a kaleidoscope of color when it comes to my scarves. This love affair began many, many years ago, and as of late, has seen a reawakening. There is something about silk; perhaps it is the way it feels, so soft to the touch. Or maybe it's the way it drapes so perfectly around my neck, or that it can elevate my ensemble so effortlessly. So much power one small scarf possesses!
