Lovely Ladies Perks Program

We pride ourselves on bringing to your doorstep luxury fashion curated from only the best international designers. We are selective in what materials and garments we bring through our doors and as such wanted to outline our policies and Lovely Ladies Perks program.

We are a small boutique and as such do not accept returns. Exchanges can be made within seven days of purchase. If you are purchasing from our e-shop, be sure to inspect all photos carefully and read all descriptions before checking out. We want you to absolutely love your purchase. Please feel free to email us with questions or stop by our boutique to get a closer look at items that you have flirted with online.

We will have two sales a year during the last week of March and August. As we order very limited quantities, we encourage you to grab must-have apparel and accessories, as we are unable to restock them and most of these pieces will never make it to sale.

Our Lovely Ladies Perks Program is our way of saying thank you! On the anniversary of your first purchase from LILA, you will enjoy a special discount from us. This date will be tracked at the time of your first purchase when we set up your customer profile. Additionally, every time you spend $35 or more at LILA, you'll earn a star. After 5 stars you'll receive a 15% off coupon that is redeemable in store.
